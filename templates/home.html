Welcome to the Structure and Dynamics Factors Simulator

This application allows you to calculate and graph physical properties such as the structure factor and diffusion of a system based on parameters such as particle size, volume fraction (ϕ), and reduced temperature (T*).

Instructions

  • Enter the radius of the large and small particles.
  • Select values for ϕ (phi) and T* (reduced temperature).
  • Choose whether to calculate structure or dynamics.
  • Press Submit to generate results.
